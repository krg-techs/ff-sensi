🎯 Training Module — Pro Tips
⚠ Advisory: These are optimized starting points. Tweak each value by ±5 points in Training Grounds to perfect your muscle memory.
◈ Daily Muscle Memory Protocol ◈
🎯 Master the Red Numbers
10-Minute Daily Routine Before Every Ranked Session
01
The "Feel" Warm-Up
⏱ 2 Minutes
ACTIONEnter Training Grounds with no weapon.
GOALSprint and jump while flicking your screen 180° back and forth.
WHYGets your thumb calibrated to General sensitivity. Cold thumbs miss — don't skip this.
02
The "Straight Drag" Technique
⏱ 3 Minutes
ACTIONPick up an AR (M4A1 or AK47). Stand still, aim at bot's chest.
GOALDrag your fire button upward in a straight line.
⚡ PRO TIP
Don't "rip" the button. Use a smooth, medium-speed swipe to let auto-aim lock onto the head. Speed kills accuracy here.
03
The "Rotation Drag"
⏱ 3 Minutes
ACTIONEquip Shotgun (M1887 or Mag-7). Close the distance to the bot.
GOALMove fire button in a "J" shape — down then up.
The "J" drag — secret to headshots on moving / close-range targets
WHYThe curve compensates for enemy lateral movement. Use this for shotgun rush plays.
04
Sniper Flick Practice
⏱ 2 Minutes
ACTIONUse the AWM or M82B.
GOALQuick-scope and flick to target. Aim for head zone in one motion.
🔧 ADJUSTMENT
Overshooting? Lower Sniper Scope by −5 points.
Too slow? Raise it by +5 points.
⚠
THE GOLDEN CALIBRATION RULE
HITTING BODY ONLY → Increase General by +5
CROSSHAIR FLYING OVER HEAD → Decrease General by −5
Tweak in ±5 increments only. Never change more than one setting at a time. Give each change 200 shots before judging.
