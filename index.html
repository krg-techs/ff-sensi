🎯 Pro Tips For Your Settings

⚠ Note: These are starting points. Go to Training Grounds and adjust each value by ±5 until it feels right for you.

🎯 10-Min Daily Training

Do this before every ranked session

STEP 01

Feel Warm-Up

⏱ 2 Minutes

Enter Training Grounds with no weapon. Sprint and jump while doing 180° flick swipes back and forth across the screen. This wakes up your thumbs and gets them synced to your General sensitivity.

STEP 02

Straight Drag Headshots

⏱ 3 Minutes

Pick up an AR (M4A1 or AK47). Aim at a bot's chest and drag your fire button straight upward in one smooth motion. Use a medium-speed swipe — not too fast. The drag should carry the crosshair from chest to head automatically.

STEP 03

J-Shape Drag (Close Range)

⏱ 3 Minutes

Equip a Shotgun (M1887). Get close to the bot. Instead of dragging straight up, drag your fire button in a "J" shape — down first, then curve upward. This is the secret technique for hitting headshots on moving enemies at close range.

STEP 04

Sniper Flick Practice

⏱ 2 Minutes

Use the AWM or M82B. Quick-scope and flick to the target in one motion aiming for the head. If you keep overshooting → lower Sniper −5. If you feel too slow → raise Sniper +5.

⚠ The Golden Rule

Hitting body only? → Increase General by +5
Crosshair flying over head? → Decrease General by −5

Only change one setting at a time. Give it 200 shots before judging.
